Yoga, Pilates & Tai Chi 
Course name
Flow and Restore Yoga*
A class that brings together breath, body and mind through the practice of asana. Class ends with restorative poses to revive your vital energy - calming, healing and soothing way to end your practice.
Yoga for Hips and Shoulders*
This class will focus on movement and poses designed to strengthen and increase a range of motion in the hips and shoulders, as well as to help develop greater body awareness. Great for beginners and advanced students alike.
Pilates
Pilates is a series of movements designed to strengthen, tone and improve flexibility. Using isolated movements, you will feel the burn and work towards a long, lean body.
Tai Chi Foundations
The instructor will teach a set of standing exercises which will provide experience in grounding, Silk reeling and traditional Yang style movements. Tai Chi can help increase flexibility, muscle strength and feeling of well-being. Yang style is suitable for all ages and abilities.
